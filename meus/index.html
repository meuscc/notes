集合论

定义

外延

$$ S = \{x, y, z, ... \} $$

内涵

$$ S = \{x | P(x) \} $$

关系

$$ x \in S $$

$$ x \subseteq S $$

定理

$$ A \subseteq B \land B \subseteq C \implies A \subseteq C $$

$$ A \subseteq B \land B \subseteq A \implies A = B $$

函词

$$ A \cap B; A \cup B; A - B; \complement A $$

$$ A \odot B; A \oplus B $$

定理

$$ A-B = A \cap \complement B $$

$$ A \oplus B = (A - B) \cup (B - A) $$

定理

$$ A \oplus B = (A \cap \complement B) \cup (\complement A \cap B) = (A \cup B) \cap (\complement A \cup \complement B) $$

$$ A \odot B = (A \cap B) \cup (\complement A \cap \complement B) = (A \cup \complement B) \cap (\complement A \cup B) $$
